About Steffen Giessner

Steffen R. Giessner is Professor of Organisational Behaviour and Change at Rotterdam School of Management, Erasmus University.

Profile

Steffen's research is located at the intersection of organisational psychology and management. He studies how people experience leadership, identity, power, and change in organisations, with particular attention to mergers, leader behaviour, and leadership impact.

He holds an MSc in Psychology from the University of Kent at Canterbury and a PhD in Psychology from Friedrich Schiller University Jena. His work has appeared in leading outlets in organisational behaviour, management, and psychology, and has received international media attention.

Research Interests

  • Leadership and leader behaviour
  • Organisational change and mergers
  • Follower perceptions of leadership
  • Non-verbal communication of power and presence
