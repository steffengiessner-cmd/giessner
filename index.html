Academic profile

Leadership, organisational change, and the human side of work.

Steffen R. Giessner is Professor of Organisational Behaviour and Change at Rotterdam School of Management, Erasmus University. His work connects organisational psychology and management, with a focus on leadership, mergers, identity, and non-verbal leadership impact.
