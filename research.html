Research

Research Agenda

Steffen's research examines leadership, identity, power, and organisational change through the lenses of organisational psychology and management.

Leadership

Follower Perceptions and Leader Behaviour

This stream studies how people perceive leaders, what shapes leader behaviour, and how leadership relationships affect employee outcomes.

Change

Organisational Mergers and Employee Support

A central theme in Steffen's work is how employees navigate organisational mergers, identity change, and uncertainty, and how supportive leadership can improve outcomes.

Presence

Non-Verbal Communication of Power

This research explores how non-verbal behaviour, body language, and voice contribute to leadership presence, power, and impact in face-to-face and online settings.
