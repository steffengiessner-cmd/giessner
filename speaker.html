Speaker

Leadership Presence, Change, and Organisational Behaviour

Steffen speaks on leadership, organisational change, non-verbal communication, hybrid work, and the behavioural dynamics that shape how people experience organisations.

Speaking Themes

  • Leading with presence in face-to-face, hybrid, and online settings
  • How non-verbal communication shapes leadership impact
  • Supporting employees through mergers and organisational change
  • Follower perceptions, identity, power, and trust in leadership
  • Making virtual meetings and hybrid collaboration more effective

Media and Public Engagement

Steffen's work has been covered by international outlets including the New York Times, Financial Times, The Guardian, Harvard Business Review, Harvard Business Manager, and the Wall Street Journal.

Recent media topics include hybrid working, effective online meetings, leader-member relationships, and leadership presence.
